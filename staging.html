Our Staging Process

Step 1: Initial Consultation

We visit your property to assess space, style, and target buyer demographics. We'll discuss your timeline, budget, and goals.

Step 2: Custom Staging Plan

Receive a detailed proposal with furniture layouts, color schemes, and a complete inventory of items we'll use.

Step 3: Furniture & Décor Selection

Our team curates furniture and accessories that complement your home's architecture and appeal to your target market.

Step 4: Professional Installation

Staging crew delivers and arranges all items, typically completed in 1-2 days. We handle every detail so you don't lift a finger.

Step 5: Final Styling

Finishing touches including artwork placement, fresh flowers, and adjustments to ensure magazine-worthy presentation.

Step 6: Photography & Listing

Coordinate with your photographer for optimal timing. Once sold, we'll de-stage the property and remove all items.
