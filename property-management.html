Our Property Management Process

1

Property Assessment & Onboarding

We inspect your property, photograph every room, review condition, and recommend any repairs or improvements to maximize rental value. We'll also determine optimal rental pricing based on current market data.

2

Marketing & Tenant Search

Professional photos, compelling listing descriptions, and multi-platform advertising (Zillow, Apartments.com, Craigslist, social media). We schedule and conduct showings, answer inquiries, and pre-screen interested applicants.

3

Tenant Screening & Selection

Comprehensive background checks, credit reports, employment verification, and rental history. We present qualified candidates to you for final approval with our recommendation.

4

Lease Signing & Move-In

Execute state-compliant lease agreements, collect first month's rent and security deposit, conduct detailed move-in inspection with photos, and provide tenant orientation on property features and policies.

5

Ongoing Management & Support

Monthly rent collection, maintenance coordination, routine inspections, lease renewals, and regular communication. You receive monthly statements and have 24/7 access to your owner portal.

6

Move-Out & Turnover

Conduct move-out inspection, process security deposit disposition per state law, coordinate any necessary repairs or cleaning, and immediately begin marketing for the next tenant.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you rent my property?

Our average time to lease is 14-21 days from listing. Properties priced correctly and in good condition typically lease faster. We'll provide a competitive market analysis to ensure optimal pricing.

Do I have to approve tenants?

Yes! We present all qualified applicants with our professional recommendation, but you always have final approval. We'll never place a tenant without your consent.

What if my tenant doesn't pay rent?

We enforce late fees per the lease agreement and immediately begin collection efforts. If necessary, we'll coordinate the eviction process (legal fees separate). Our thorough screening minimizes this risk.

How do I receive my rental income?

Rent is deposited directly to your bank account by the 10th of each month. You'll receive a detailed statement showing all income and expenses via our online owner portal.

What's the lease term?

Standard leases are 12 months. We can accommodate 6-month leases if preferred, though annual leases provide better stability. Month-to-month is also available at higher rates.

Can I cancel management services?

Yes. We require 60 days written notice. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation penalties. We're confident you'll love our service, but flexibility is important to us.
