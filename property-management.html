Our Partnership Process

1

Initial Consultation

Schedule a free consultation to discuss your property, investment goals, current challenges, and expectations. We'll provide an honest assessment of whether we're a good fit and answer all your questions about our services.

2

Property Assessment

We'll tour your property (or review plans for new construction), evaluate current operations if applicable, assess maintenance needs, and conduct a comprehensive market analysis to benchmark performance.

3

Custom Proposal

Receive a detailed management proposal including pricing specific to your property type, recommended services, marketing strategy, improvement recommendations, and projected financial performance. Delivered within 48 hours.

4

Agreement & Transition

Sign the management agreement and we begin the transition process: tenant file review, vendor coordination, bank account setup, system implementation, and communication with existing tenants (if applicable).

5

Launch & Operations

Full management begins with your dedicated account manager. For occupied properties, we take over rent collection, maintenance, and tenant relations. For new builds, we execute the lease-up marketing campaign.

6

Ongoing Partnership

Regular performance reports, proactive property maintenance, continuous optimization, and direct access to decision-makers. We grow your investment value through strategic management.

Common Questions From Property Owners

Do you currently manage properties?

We're launching our property management division and actively seeking founding partners. This means you'll receive dedicated, personalized service and preferential pricing as we build this part of our business. Our team brings extensive real estate experience and strong industry relationships.

What size properties do you work with?

We specialize in multi-family properties from small duplexes to large apartment complexes (50+ units). We're particularly interested in partnering with developers on new-build projects and investors with multi-unit portfolios. Single-family homes are not our focus.

Can you help with new construction lease-up?

Absolutely! We offer specialized lease-up services including pre-construction planning, marketing strategy, rapid tenant placement, and bulk move-in coordination. We can start working with you before construction is complete.

What makes VDI different from other property managers?

We focus exclusively on multi-family properties, offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees, use modern technology platforms, and provide dedicated account management. As founding partners, you'll have direct access to decision-makers, not just an account rep.

How quickly can you take over management?

We can typically transition existing properties within 30 days. This includes property assessment, tenant file review, financial account setup, and vendor onboarding. For new construction, we can begin marketing 60-90 days before completion.

Do you require long-term contracts?

Our standard agreement is 12 months with 60-day notice for cancellation. We believe in earning your business through excellent service, not trapping you in long-term contracts. Flexibility is important to us.

What technology do you use?

We use industry-leading property management software with online portals for owners and tenants, digital rent collection, maintenance request tracking, and comprehensive reporting. Everything is accessible 24/7 from your computer or mobile device.

How do I get started?

Schedule a free consultation below. We'll discuss your property, review your goals, provide a market analysis, and create a custom management proposal. No obligation - just an honest conversation about whether we're a good fit.
